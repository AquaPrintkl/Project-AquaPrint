Objective

At Aqua Print Solutions, our mission is to leverage innovative 3D printing techniques to revitalize and restore coral reefs worldwide, contributing to the health of our oceans and the preservation of marine life.

What is ——

AquaPrint


Restore 20% of Malaysia's critically damaged coral reefs by 2028 through innovative 3D printed structures, fostering marine biodiversity and empowering local communities.

  • We utilize cutting-edge 3D printing technology to create intricate structures that mimic natural coral formations.
  • These structures provide crucial habitat for fish and marine life, accelerating reef regeneration.
  • Our approach is cost-effective and scalable, allowing for widespread restoration efforts.
  • We partner with local communities to ensure cultural sensitivity and build capacity for long-term reef management.
Collection
Gather discarded plastic bottles from events, oceans and shorelines.
Processing
Recycle plastic into filaments for 3D printing coral structures.
Deployment
Place printed corals in strategic areas to support marine life.
